Return Policy

BDClassics will gladly take any return within 30 days. Follow these three steps:

  1. Notify our team of the return by contacting us at Contact Us

    2. BDClassics will email you to confirm the return.

  2. Ship the product back with all of the original parts.

    3. Shipping returns is the responsibility of you the customer and BDClassics will pay to ship a replacement to you. Please include all of the original parts with the return.

  3. Our team will quickly ship the replacement or refund the payment.

    4. BDClassics works to keep a goal of service for replacements. You will recieve an email when the return is processed to notify you of your refund or replacement tracking number. If you choose to take a refund we will refund you minus our costs for shipping.
